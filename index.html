About Shree Weaves

Rooted in Surat, woven for the world

From the textile capital of India, we specialise in crafting premium fabrics that bridge artisanal detail with industrial precision — serving fashion houses, export buyers, and boutique labels alike.

Founded in Surat, Gujarat, Shree Weaves brings together over 15 years of manufacturing expertise with a simple philosophy — every metre of fabric should tell a story of intention, craftsmanship, and consistency.

Our mills are equipped with advanced looms, in-house design teams, and stringent quality controls that ensure your collections launch on time, at global standards, every time.

Design-Led Manufacturing Collaborate with our studio to develop exclusive weaves, textures, and colour stories tailored to your brand.
Export Ready Operations End-to-end documentation, sampling, and logistics support for buyers across Europe, Middle East & USA.
Surat · Gujarat Where looms never sleep.
